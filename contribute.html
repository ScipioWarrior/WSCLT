Here's how you can help WSCLT achieve its goal and ensure affordable housing:


Become a member

Types of memberships:
  • Individual (within 28208, 28214, 28216, 28217) $20
  • Individual (outside above ZIPs) $50
  • Organizational (budget <$150,000) $150
  • Organizational (budget $150,000-500,000) $300
  • Organizational (budget >$500,000) $500


Attend Board Meetings

  • West Side CLT board of directors meetings are open to anyone.
  • Calendar coming soon!
